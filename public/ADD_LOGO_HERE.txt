LOGO SETUP INSTRUCTIONS
========================

Please save the NyayaLens AI logo image you provided as:

Filename: logo.png
Location: This folder (nyayalens-ai/public/)

Steps:
1. Download/save the NyayaLens AI logo image
2. Rename it to: logo.png
3. Place it in: nyayalens-ai/public/logo.png
4. Refresh your browser at http://localhost:3000/dashboard

The logo will automatically appear in:
- Top-left corner of the dashboard navbar
- All pages with the DashboardNavbar component

Expected dimensions: 400x100 pixels (or similar aspect ratio)
Format: PNG with transparent background (recommended)

Once added, you can delete this instruction file.
